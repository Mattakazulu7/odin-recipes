Pesto Pasta

Description

Pesto Pasta is a delicious Italian dish that combines al dente pasta with a flavorful basil pesto sauce. It's quick and easy to make, making it a perfect weeknight dinner option. The vibrant green color of the pesto sauce adds a beautiful touch to the dish, and the aromatic basil and garlic infuse the pasta with amazing flavor. Serve it with a sprinkle of parmesan cheese for an extra indulgence.

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta (such as spaghetti or penne)
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a food processor, combine basil leaves, parmesan cheese, pine nuts, and garlic. Pulse until finely chopped.
  3. With the food processor running, slowly pour in the olive oil. Continue blending until the mixture forms a smooth paste.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta with the pesto sauce until well coated. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot, optionally garnished with extra parmesan cheese.
