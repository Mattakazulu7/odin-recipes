Alfredo Fettuccine

Description

Alfredo Fettuccine is a rich and creamy Italian pasta dish that features silky smooth fettuccine noodles smothered in a decadent Alfredo sauce. This indulgent dish is perfect for special occasions or when you're craving comfort food. The creamy sauce, made with butter, cream, and parmesan cheese, creates a luxurious texture that coats every strand of pasta.

Ingredients

  • 300g fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the heavy cream and stir until heated through.
  3. Add the grated parmesan cheese and continue to stir until the sauce is smooth and well combined.
  4. Toss the cooked fettuccine with the Alfredo sauce until well coated. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot, optionally garnished with extra parmesan cheese and freshly ground black pepper.
